What We Offer

Our Clock Repair Services

Every service is performed personally by Jerry Lindell — a second-generation horologist with over 60 years of experience and the tools to handle any clock, any condition.

⚙️
Movement Repair & Overhaul

Full disassembly, ultrasonic cleaning, inspection, and reassembly of clock movements. We identify worn or broken parts and restore proper function to movements that have stopped, run too fast or slow, or make grinding noises.

🔧
Lubrication & Precision Tuning

Precision oiling of all pivot points, mainsprings, and gear trains using period-appropriate lubricants. Proper lubrication dramatically extends movement life and improves timekeeping accuracy. Recommended every 3–5 years.

🏺
Antique & Heirloom Restoration

Sensitive restoration of antique grandfather clocks, tall case clocks, and family heirlooms. We preserve the original character and authenticity of your clock while returning it to proper working condition.

🔩
Custom Parts Fabrication

When antique clock parts aren't commercially available — which is common — Jerry machines them from scratch on-site using professional lathes, mills, and specialized clockmaking tools. No part is beyond our capability.

🚚
Professional Clock Relocation

Grandfather clocks must be professionally packed before any move. We handle local area moves and select long-distance moves along the entire I-95 corridor, from Florida to Philadelphia. Cheaper than repairing a damaged clock.

🏠
In-Home Service Appointments

For clocks that cannot be easily moved, Jerry comes to you. Available throughout our full service area, from Florida to the Philadelphia metro area. Evening appointments available on a limited basis.

🎵
Chime & Strike Repair

Repair of Westminster chime mechanisms, hour-strike trains, and quarter-hour systems. We also address chimes that strike the wrong hour, run ahead or behind, or have become out of sequence.

📦
Case Restoration Referrals

While our specialty is the clock movement, we work with trusted furniture restoration partners for case repair, refinishing, and glass replacement. We coordinate the whole process for you.
