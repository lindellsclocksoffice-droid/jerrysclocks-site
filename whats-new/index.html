Jerry Built an Entire Grandfather Clock Movement by Hand
Using his decades of clockmaking skill, Jerry Lindell built a complete grandfather clock movement from scratch — every single part machined by hand in his Fort Lauderdale workshop. In some cases, he even had to fabricate the tools needed just to make certain components. It's a demonstration of mastery that very few clockmakers in the country could match.
