Trusted Clock Repair for Rockville Homes

Lindell's brings over 33 years of horology experience to Rockville and the surrounding Maryland area. Whether your grandfather clock has stopped running, needs routine lubrication, or requires a full antique restoration, every repair is handled personally by Jerry himself, backed by a team that takes care of scheduling and customer service.

As a 1964 graduate of the Baronian School of Clock and Watch Making and a second-generation craftsman, Jerry has the rare ability to fabricate custom parts on-site when originals aren't available — meaning no clock in Rockville is beyond repair.
