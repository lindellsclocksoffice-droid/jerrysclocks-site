Our Services
Everything Your Clock Needs
From routine lubrication to full movement rebuilds, every repair is performed personally by Jerry Lindell, with a team behind the scenes handling scheduling and customer service.
Movement Repair & Overhaul

Full disassembly, cleaning, and reassembly. We restore proper function to stopped, noisy, or misbehaving movements.

Lubrication & Tuning

Precision oiling of all pivot points and mainsprings to extend movement life and improve accuracy.

Antique Clock Restoration

Sensitive restoration of heirloom and collectible grandfather clocks — authenticity preserved, function restored.

Custom Parts Fabrication

When parts aren't commercially available, Jerry machines them from scratch using lathes and mills on-site.

Safe Clock Relocation

Professional packing and moving along the I-95 corridor. Less expensive than repairing damage from a bad move.

In-Home Service Calls

We come to you. Evening appointments available on a limited basis across our full service area.
